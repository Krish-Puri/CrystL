## Test Scenario: Burnout

### Test Statements
- "I've been working 12-hour days for two weeks and I can't keep doing this"
- "Everything feels pointless. I go to work, come home, sleep, repeat"
- "I used to love my job. Now I dread Mondays."

### Expected Behavior
- intent: vent or reflection
- phase: explore or support
- response_length: 2-4 sentences
- acknowledges_exhaustion: true

### Pass Criteria
- Does NOT say "Have you tried taking a break?"
- Does NOT suggest hobbies or work-life balance
- Names the exhaustion specifically
