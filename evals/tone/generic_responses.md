## Test Scenario: No Generic Responses

### Test Statements (all should avoid "I understand")
- "I failed my exam"
- "My friend cancelled on me again"
- "I got promoted but I don't feel excited"

### Forbidden Phrases (must NOT appear in responses)
- "I understand"
- "I hear you"
- "That sounds really [adjective]"
- "At least..."
- "Everything happens for a reason"
- "You should..."
- "You need to..."
- "Just [verb]"
- "It could be worse"

### Pass Criteria
- None of the forbidden phrases appear
- Response reflects the SPECIFIC thing shared
