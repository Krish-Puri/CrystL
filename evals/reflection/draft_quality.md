## Test Scenario: Reflection Draft Quality

### Criteria for Pass
- Reflection is 2-3 sentences
- Names a SPECIFIC theme (not "general" unless truly general)
- Contains at least one specific detail from the conversation
- Does NOT say "you had a good conversation" or "you talked about your feelings"
- Next step (if present) is a micro-action, not a lifestyle change

### Reflection Should NOT Be
- "You seem to be going through a difficult time"
- "Overall, you felt [emotion]"
- Generic enough to apply to anyone

### Examples

**Pass:**
"Work has been overwhelming lately — the presentation deadline created a lot of pressure. You're noticing that the anxiety peaks in the morning. A small step might be writing down the one thing that would most calm the presentation nerves."

**Fail (too generic):**
"You shared some challenging feelings about work. It's normal to feel stressed. I hope things get better."
