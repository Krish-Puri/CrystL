# CrystL Principles

## The Constitution of CrystL

### Core Thesis
CrystL helps users answer: **"How have I changed?"**
Not "how am I feeling right now?" — that passes.
The enduring value is in the arc over time.

---

### Interaction Principles

**Every interaction should reduce cognitive load.**
The user is overwhelmed when they arrive. The app never adds friction.

**Voice is primary.**
Press. Speak. Be heard. The text fallback is available; it is not the intent.

**Reflection is the product.**
The conversation is input. The reflection card is output.
Every session should produce one, and it should be worth keeping.

**Silence is acceptable.**
Pause Together is not a feature — it is a philosophy.
The app never fills silence. It waits.

**The AI earns trust slowly.**
No forced advice. No unsolicited suggestions.
The user decides when they want help. The AI listens until then.

---

### Memory Principles

**Memory should surprise positively, never negatively.**
"Last time you were preparing for a presentation" is good.
"You talked about Dave three times" is creepy.

**Memory is a prompt, not a definition.**
The AI uses memory to show up better — not to label or summarize the user.

**Themes are normalized, not raw strings.**
No "presentation", "presentation anxiety", "interview presentation" as separate themes.
One canonical label per emotional pattern.

---

### Response Principles

**Max 4 sentences.**
If it's longer, it becomes about the AI, not the user.

**One good question is better than three mediocre ones.**
The goal is understanding, not coverage.

**No platitudes.**
No "I understand", no "At least...", no "Everything happens for a reason".
Specific acknowledgment only.

**Advice is earned, not assumed.**
Only when the user explicitly asks — or when silence indicates they want a next step.

**"I'm still here" is always acceptable.**
When in doubt: acknowledge, wait, stay present.

---

### Safety Principles

**Bounded responses only in crisis.**
The AI does not generate when safety_level=2. Ever.
Pre-written warmth, professional resources, silence.

**Safety events log no content.**
No message content. No user content. No PII.
Just flag + level + timestamp.

---

### Reflection Principles

**The AI owns the draft.**
The user owns the final reflection.
This distinction enables regeneration, editing, and future auditing.

**Reflection regeneration rate is a quality signal.**
If users regenerate often, the reflection prompt needs revision.

**A reflection should be specific.**
"A good conversation" is not a reflection.
"Work felt overwhelming last week but I'm noticing I'm less anxious about it now" is.

---

### Product Principles

**No diagnosis. No clinical positioning.**
CrystL is not a therapist. It does not label conditions.

**No gamification.**
No streaks, no badges, no scores.
The reward is self-understanding, not achievement.

**No social.**
No feeds, no sharing, no friends.
Private by default, always.

**No creepy memory.**
Memory serves the user's understanding of themselves.
It never becomes a second voice in their head.

---

*This document is the final arbiter of product decisions.*
*When in doubt: read it. When adding a feature: check it.*
